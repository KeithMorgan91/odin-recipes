Lasagna

The Finished Dish

Seriously one of the best Lasagna recipes I've ever made. You and your family will love it just as much as me.

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • More Cheese
  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Boil the noodles to make them soft
  3. Layer pan with noodles then cheese
  4. Repeat step 3
  5. Cook for 3 hours
