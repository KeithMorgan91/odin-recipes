Chicken Noodle Soup

The Finished Dish

So simple and so tasty. Just what the Dr. ordered on a cold cloudy day!

  • Egg Noodles
  • Chicken broth
  • Basil
  • Salt & Pepper
  1. Boil 2 cups of water, add noodles
  2. Boil the noodles to make them soft
  3. Strain noodles and add hot broth
  4. Sprinkle with seasonings
  5. Enjoy!
