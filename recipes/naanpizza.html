Naan Pizza

The Finished Dish

Everyone loves pizza night. This dish is so fast and tasty at the same time!

  • 4 Slices of Naan
  • Pizza Sauce
  • 1C Mozarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Layer the Naan evenly with sauce
  3. Add the Mozarella cheese
  4. Top with Pepperoni
  5. Bake for 8-10 minutes
