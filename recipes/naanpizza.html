The Finished Dish

Everyone loves pizza night. This dish is so fast and tasty at the same time!

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • More Cheese
  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Boil the noodles to make them soft
  3. Layer pan with noodles then cheese
  4. Repeat step 3
  5. Cook for 3 hours
